Why It Matters

The hidden cost of disconnected systems

Most hospitals don't lose money in one big way — they lose it in a hundred small leaks between departments that never share the same data. Adamrit closes each one.

Running on registers & silos

The everyday reality without a connected system

✕

Revenue leaks — medicines, consumables, and scans get delivered but never reach the bill.

✕

Slow discharges — final bills are assembled by hand from scattered slips, holding beds for hours.

✕

Claim rejections — CGHS/PMJAY/ESIC packages priced wrong, documents missing, money stuck for months.

✕

No live visibility — directors learn the month's numbers weeks after the month has ended.

✕

Stock & expiry losses — pharmacy write-offs nobody saw coming.

✕

Reconciliation pain — billing, accounts, and Tally never quite agree.

Running on Adamrit

One system, one source of truth

✓

Every charge captured — each service posts to the visit bill the moment it's delivered.

✓

One-click discharge bills — assembled automatically from real activity, ready in minutes.

✓

First-time-right claims — built-in scheme rates, package codes, and auto-generated documents.

✓

Live director dashboard — revenue, occupancy, and referrals update as they happen.

✓

Batch-level stock control — expiry alerts, low-stock alerts, full movement audit.

✓

Books that tie out — billing flows into double-entry accounts and syncs to Tally.
