# Glossary

- **Template** — `appointment_type_templates` row keyed by appointment type. Single source of truth for prep steps.
- **OPD** — Outpatient Department.
- **IPD** — Inpatient Department (admitted patients).
- **Fasting** — no food or water for the specified hours; check whether water-allowed in the template.
- **ESIC** — Employees' State Insurance Corporation; affects which forms patient brings, not the prep steps.

## Languages
- `en` — English. Default.
- `hi` — Hindi. Devanagari script. Use formal "aap" form.
- `mr` — Marathi. Devanagari script. Use polite forms.

## Tone
Warm but functional. The reader is anxious about a hospital visit. Keep it clear and short.
